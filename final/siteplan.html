Site Plan: Simple Plate

Site Name

Simple Plate. This name was chosen because it directly communicates the website's core purpose: providing simple, uncomplicated recipes. It's memorable, easy to say, and evokes a sense of clean, straightforward cooking.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Simple Plate is to be a minimalist, no-frills recipe finder for users who want to cook with what they already have. The site will help busy professionals, students, and beginner cooks quickly find simple recipes based on a single core ingredient, removing the hassle of complex meal planning and grocery shopping.

Scenarios

  • "I have chicken breasts in the fridge that I need to use tonight. What's a quick and easy recipe I can make with minimal extra ingredients?"
  • "I'm new to cooking and find most recipe websites overwhelming. Where can I find a simple dish that won't take me hours to prepare?"

Color Schema

  • #F8F9FA (Cultured): A very light, off-white for the main page background. It's softer on the eyes than pure white and provides a clean, airy feel.
  • #1e5a21 (Dark Green): A fresh, inviting green to be used for headings, links, buttons, and other interactive elements. This color suggests freshness, health, and simplicity.
  • #333333 (Onyx): A dark gray for all body text. It provides strong contrast against the light background for excellent readability without the harshness of pure black.
  • #FFFFFF (White): Used for the background of cards and modal dialogs to make them stand out against the slightly off-white page background.

Typography

  • Montserrat: This clean, modern sans-serif font will be used for all headings (h1, h2). Its geometric shapes give a professional and organized look.
  • Roboto: This highly readable and friendly sans-serif font will be used for all body text, including paragraphs and lists. It's a workhorse font known for its clarity on digital screens.
